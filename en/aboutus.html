About Us

Çiçekli Artificial Intelligence Community, founded in 2023 in Çiçekli, Izmir, aims to introduce people to artificial intelligence, to spread and expand the usage areas of artificial intelligence, starting from high school level. It also aims to accelerate its spread by organizing educational content, meetings and interviews. The community consists of 3 main sections. These are education, media and R&D respectively. The education committee is responsible for planning, preparing and presenting educational content and meetings. The media committee undertakes the preparation and sharing of content together with the education committee. Thus, the trainings are made open to everyone. It also organizes events by contacting competent people in the field. The R&D committee carries out the more technical work that needs to be handled for the trainings in the background, such as research, code preparation, data set organization. In addition, the people in charge of this committee are also effective in supporting the members by sharing their experiences with them.
